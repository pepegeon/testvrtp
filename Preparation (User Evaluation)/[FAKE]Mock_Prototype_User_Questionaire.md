📋 XR User Study – Survey Form Design

Surveys used: IPQ + SSQ + GEQ (short, cherry-picked)

🧱 Overall Structure (IMPORTANT)
Do NOT mix questions randomly.
Order matters to reduce bias and fatigue.
Recommended Order

Participant & session info
SSQ (cybersickness – immediate bodily state)
IPQ (presence – reflective)
GEQ (short) (flow / engagement)
Open-ended probes (2–3 only)

Why this order?
SSQ captures physical symptoms before memory fades
IPQ captures spatial & experiential reflection
GEQ captures game-like engagement
Probes explain why scores look the way they do

🤢 Section 2: Simulator Sickness Questionnaire (SSQ)
Instruction text (important):
Please rate how much you experienced the following symptoms during or after the XR experience.

Scale (standard):
0 = None
1 = Slight
2 = Moderate
3 = Severe

🌐 Section 3: Igroup Presence Questionnaire (IPQ)

Instruction text:

Please indicate how much you agree with the following statements about your experience.

Scale (standard IPQ):

1 = Strongly disagree

7 = Strongly agree

IPQ Items (14 total)

Spatial Presence
1. I felt like I was really there in the environment.
2. I had a sense of being present in the virtual space.
3. I felt surrounded by the virtual environment.

Involvement
4. I was completely captivated by the experience.
5. I paid more attention to the virtual environment than to the real world.
6. I was deeply involved in the experience.

Realism
7. The virtual environment seemed realistic.
8. The experience felt consistent with the real world.
9. The environment responded realistically to my actions.

General Presence
10. I felt present in the virtual environment.
11. I had the impression of actually being in the environment.
12. The environment felt convincing.
13. I felt immersed in the experience.
14. I felt detached from the real world. (reverse-scored)

👉 Make sure you note reverse scoring in your analysis section.

🎮 Section 4: GEQ – Short Form (Flow & Engagement)

You do NOT want the full GEQ.
Below is a safe, commonly accepted short subset (6 items).

Instruction text:

Please indicate how much the following statements describe your experience.

Scale (GEQ standard):

0 = Not at all

4 = Extremely

GEQ (Selected Items)

I felt completely absorbed in the experience.

I lost track of time while playing.

I felt skilful during the experience.

I felt challenged at the right level.

I enjoyed the experience.

I felt in control of my actions.

👉 This gives you:
absorption
challenge–skill balance
enjoyment
agency
→ perfect for game-like XR apps



🧠 Section 5: Open-Ended Probes (MAX 3)
These are where your study becomes insightful.
Use exactly 2–3, no more.
Recommended Probes

“What moment felt the most immersive to you?”
“Did anything break your immersion or pull you out of the experience?”
“How did movement or interaction affect your comfort or engagement?”

👉 These answers:
explain IPQ score changes
contextualize SSQ symptoms
link system parameters to experience
